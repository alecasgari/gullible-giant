## Project Overview

The project involved the development and implementation of a full-scale Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system for Pharma Tech, a pharmaceutical company. Led by Dr. Herfan Pakchian, the system was designed specifically to meet the complex demands of the pharmaceutical industry, including drug import, export, and production management for all company departments.

## Problem Statement

Pharma Tech required a unified system to manage operations across various departments such as commerce, sales, technical services, after-sales service, and logistics. The absence of an integrated workflow hampered efficiency and operational visibility.

## Solution

* Developed a customized ERP solution encompassing all necessary forms, workflows, and operational cycles vital for pharmaceutical industry business processes.
* Integrated workflow automation using N8N connector technology to facilitate seamless task handoffs and approval processes among involved personnel.
* Implemented dynamic PDF previews generated through multiple programming languages to enhance documentation and process tracking.
* Covered all departmental needs from procurement to logistics ensuring total operational integration.

## Technologies Used

* N8N for automation and connectors
* Multiple programming languages for dynamic PDF generation

## Results

* The fully integrated ERP system resulted in a significant operational efficiency improvement.
* Enabled Pharma Tech to increase sales by 40%, marking a major business growth milestone.
